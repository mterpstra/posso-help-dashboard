Request Data Deletion (Right to Erasure)

We respect your privacy rights. If you wish to exercise your **Right to Erasure** (also known as the right to be forgotten) and have all your personal data permanently deleted from the ZapManejo application, please use the button below to send us an email request.

This action will initiate the deletion of all personal data associated with your account, including your **Name, Email, PhoneNumber, and Username**, in accordance with our data retention policy and applicable laws.

Important Note:

For security purposes, we require you to send this email from the **registered email address** associated with your ZapManejo account. We may need to contact you to verify your identity before proceeding with the deletion. Once verified, the deletion process is irreversible.
