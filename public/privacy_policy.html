Privacy Policy for ZapManejo

Last Updated: November 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how ZapManejo collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

A. Information You Directly Provide to Us

This is information you provide when you use the Service, such as when you register an account or contact us.

  • Identity and Contact Data: Username, Email, Name, and PhoneNumber. We also collect your password (hashed) for account security.
  • User Content Data: Any files (such as CSV's) you upload, post, or share on the Service.
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when you communicate with us (e.g., support emails, feedback).

B. Information We Automatically Collect

When you access and use the Service, we may automatically collect certain information from your device and your use of the Service.

  • Usage Data: Your IP address, browser type and version, the pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.
  • Tracking Technologies and Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information. We use essential cookies to maintain user sessions and preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes, including:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To operate the core functionality of the web application and manage your access.
  • To Manage Your Account: To manage your registration as a user of the Service and verify your identity using your Email and PhoneNumber.
  • To Improve the Service: To understand how our users interact with the Service so we can develop new features and fix issues.
  • To Communicate with You: To send you updates, security alerts, and support messages via your Email or PhoneNumber.
  • To Enforce our Terms and Conditions: To protect our rights and the rights of other users.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers to facilitate our Service, such as hosting providers (e.g., Heroku, AWS), database providers, or error logging services. Logging services on Digital Ocean.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).
  • With Your Consent: We may disclose your personal information for any other purpose with your consent.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We implement reasonable and appropriate security measures to protect the personal information we collect. User passwords are encrypted (hashed) before storage. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

You may have the following data protection rights, depending on your location and jurisdiction:

  • The Right to Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • The Right to Rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete (including your Name, Email, PhoneNumber, and Username).
  • The Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.
